# Use typed Step Definitions under Kit-owned macro topology

The Experiment Workflow Kit exclusively owns the v1 macro topology from Objective through confirmed serial Plan, the three-phase Step cycle, Plan completion, and Summary orchestration. Domain Packs do not provide custom workflow graphs; their Agent face supplies coherent typed Domain Step Definitions composed from narrow Preparation/Parameter Design, Execution Intent, and Result Analysis capabilities, including typed `not_required` preparation for manual Steps and typed loop intent where needed, while corresponding preconditions and Step Loop Policy remain isolated on the L2 Policy face. This keeps the shared substrate deep and enforceable without fragmenting each Step across independent registries or turning `StepDefinition` into a giant callback/service-locator record. New scientific variation uses new Step capabilities; a genuinely different topology requires a separately versioned Workflow Template.
